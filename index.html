About Me

Hello! I am an undergraduate student majoring in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Nottingham Ningbo China (UNNC), currently holding a rank of 2/65 in my cohort. My thesis focuses on SMEAC-Nav: A Semantic Memory-Enhanced Framework for Long-Horizon Embodied Navigation.
