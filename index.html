About Me

Hello! I am an undergraduate student majoring in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Nottingham Ningbo China (UNNC), and I will soon be pursuing a Master's degree in Robotics at the University of Pennsylvania (UPenn).

Research & Projects

Spatial Cognition & Semantic Navigation

Participated in the development of the Carrier-Relationship Scene Graph (CRSG) to study Open-Vocabulary Navigation in complex environments. Our method achieved an 82.4% success rate on Static Query tasks and ranked first in 90% of Long Sequence Zero-shot Navigation cases.

Foundations of Robotic Perception & Planning

Engaged in a year-long project at the University of Cambridge, focusing on robotic perception and autonomy. Additionally, led the development of SLAM technology fusing 4D LiDAR and millimeter-wave radar for low-visibility environments.

Interactive Visualization System for dToF Sensors

Independently architected and developed a frontend visualization tool using Python and PyQtGraph, enabling real-time rendering and seamless interaction with sensor data.
