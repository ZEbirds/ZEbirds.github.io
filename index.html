About

Hello! I am an undergraduate student majoring in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Nottingham Ningbo China (UNNC). I will soon be pursuing a Master's degree in Robotics at the University of Pennsylvania (UPenn).

My research focuses on Embodied AI and autonomous robot navigation. My long-term career goal is to become an industry researcher, dedicated to bridging high-level natural language instructions with low-level physical robot behaviors, ultimately developing truly user-friendly home service robots.
