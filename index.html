About Me

Hello! I am an undergraduate student majoring in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Nottingham Ningbo China (UNNC), currently holding a rank of 2/65 in my cohort. My thesis focuses on SMEAC-Nav: A Semantic Memory-Enhanced Framework for Long-Horizon Embodied Navigation. I will soon be pursuing a Master's degree in Robotics at the University of Pennsylvania (UPenn).

I am deeply passionate about Embodied AI and autonomous robot navigation. My long-term career goal is to become an industry researcher, dedicated to bridging high-level human natural language instructions with low-level physical robot behaviors, ultimately developing consumer-facing service robots that are truly user-friendly for domestic environments.
